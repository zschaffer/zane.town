Town Now Me Album Music Code

I am a 26 year old male,

born in the Pacific Northwest[1]

to a pair of loving parents,

pure creatives at heart.

I grew up with music,

always in my ears,

with obsession

for odd sounds.

My passions moved

to computers and doodads

buzzing and humming

and heating my lap.

I'd like to make tools.

Ones for artists and thinkers,

musicians and listeners,

people and children.

I'd like to make it easier,

to hear those sounds and

let people crackle,

and warble,

and pop.

---

[1] - Seattle, Washington
