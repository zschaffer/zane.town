About Now Music

You have stumbled upon my dear little siteI've been experimenting using Semantic Linefeeds as a creative writing tool

lacking much in terms of content

yet still full of life.

I'd encourage a repeat visit

as often as you'd like.

Who knows what might change

as time progresses on.

For now, may I direct your attention to

my creative endeavours.

I am a 26 year old male,

born in the Pacific Northwest

to a pair of loving parents,

pure creatives at heart.


I grew up with music,

always in my ears,

with obsession

for odd sounds.


My passions moved

to computers and doodads

buzzing and humming

and heating my lap.


I'd like to make tools.

Ones for artists and thinkers,

musicians and listeners,

people and children.


I'd like to make it easier,

to hear those sounds and

let people crackle,

and warble,

and pop.

I am currently learning more about embedded systems,

how they interact, and how they communicate inside.

I've been focusing on making things in C/C++

reading endless manpages

and tinkering with SBC's.


I've lost the motivation and free time to create

and lately I only listen.

To make up for this -

I'm starting to curate a reviewed

and detailed

listing of what I'm playing.

You can hear me stream music live

Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays

11:00am PST at tilderadio.


You can find archives of my live streams here.


My released music lives on

Spotify & Bandcamp

Apple Music & Soundcloud
