[46] Ryoji Ikeda, Mika Vainio & Alva Noto - Live 2002



++++_

The lineup on this record is of superhero movie proportions.

As far as I know, this trio has never worked on a single project,

all together, let alone a live performance.

This album moves from artsy ambient silent room echos

to pulsating, worm like bass movements

to straight up techno at times.

It is extremely hard to pin down to a specific mood

or vibe

other than 'Hell Yeah, Dude'.
